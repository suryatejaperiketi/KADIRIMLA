About Kandikunta Venkata Prasad

Kandikunta Venkata Prasad is a prominent political leader affiliated with the Telugu Desam Party (TDP), representing the Kadiri constituency in Andhra Pradesh's Anantapur district.


Development


1. Infrastructure Development

Water Supply Projects: Initiated water distribution systems to tackle drinking water issues in drought-prone regions.

Electricity Access: Enhanced access to uninterrupted power supply in villages and towns.


2.Education

Upgraded schools with better facilities such as digital classrooms and libraries.

Scholarships and financial aid for underprivileged students to encourage higher education.


3. Agriculture and Irrigation

Implementation of irrigation projects to support farmers.

Subsidies and technical support to increase agricultural productivity.


4. Health and Sanitation:

Construction of primary health centers and hospitals with modern facilities.

Awareness campaigns on hygiene and sanitation, along with constructing public toilets under Swachh Bharat initiatives.


5. Employment Opportunities

Vocational training programs for youth to increase employment opportunities.

Support for small-scale industries and self-help groups to boost local economy.


6. Social Welfare

Distribution of pensions, ration cards, and welfare benefits to eligible citizens.

Special focus on empowering women through welfare schemes and financial independence programs.


7. Environmental Initiatives:

Plantation drives to increase green cover.

Projects for groundwater recharge and conservation.


8. Accountability:

Committing to addressing corruption and improving governance in the constituency.

శాసనసభ్యులు కందికుంట వెంకటప్రసాద్ గారి ఆకస్మిక తనిఖీ – Tribal Welfare Commissioner కు ఫిర్యాదు
